Cloudiness

Cloudiness Scatter Plot

Per the scatter plot, on 01/05/17, the latitude and cloudiness have no correlation as the data are scatter throughout the plot. This indicate that latitude does not affect cloudiness, and vice versa and that both hempisphere experience cloudiness. There are a few areas where cloudiness does not affect (0%) the hempispheres and some areas where it is at a 80% cloudiness. At each 0% and 80% cloudiness, you can see the plots are within a straight line confirming that both north and south hempispheres experience the same percentages that day.
